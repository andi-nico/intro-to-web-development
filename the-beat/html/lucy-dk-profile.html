  • Bio:

    • Lucy DK seeks beauty in the search for healing and growth, her music a fusion of sleek, anecdotal pop imbued with in her own words 'a mixture of polish and chaos'. Starting from humble, Leicestershire beginnings with Zimbabwean and British heritage, and possessing big dreams, she spent her teenage years writing songs with her guitar on her bedroom floor in emulation of childhood idol Taylor Swift. A tenacious move to Nashville, aged 17, saw Lucy set aside her country endeavours after finding a home in hip hop and performance art, weaving the finesse of Solange and FKA Twigs into her own stylish delivery. This experimentation of her craft followed her to New York and later South East London, where personal upheaval led to growth as both an artist and an individual.
